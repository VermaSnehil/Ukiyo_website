What We Cover

13 Specialized Services

Every dimension of modern search — technical, content, AI, and international — handled with precision.

Full Website Audit

Comprehensive site-wide SEO health check using parallel analysis across all major ranking factors — on-page, technical, backlink profile, and content gaps — delivered as a prioritized action plan.

Deep Single-Page Analysis

Granular inspection of any individual URL — title and meta optimization, heading hierarchy, keyword density, internal linking, Core Web Vitals, and structured data validation all in one report.

Sitemap Analysis

Parses your existing XML sitemap to surface indexability issues — missing pages, blocked URLs, orphaned content, incorrect priority signals, and crawl budget waste before they cost you rankings.

Sitemap Generation

Builds production-ready XML sitemaps from industry-specific templates — tuned for SaaS, eCommerce, local business, or publisher architectures with correct priority, changefreq, and hreflang tags.

Schema.org Markup

Detects existing structured data, validates it against Google's rich-result requirements, and generates new Schema.org markup for products, services, reviews, FAQs, events, and organizations.

Image SEO Optimization

Audits every image across your site for missing alt text, oversized file weights, wrong formats, lazy-load issues, and filename conventions — with actionable fixes that improve both rankings and page speed.

Technical SEO Audit

Nine-category deep-dive covering crawlability, indexation, site speed, mobile usability, HTTPS security, canonical tags, redirect chains, structured data, and JavaScript rendering — the full technical picture.

Content Quality & E-E-A-T Analysis

Evaluates your content through Google's Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness framework — identifying thin content, missing author signals, and gaps that reduce ranking potential.

Core Service

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Optimizes your brand for AI-powered answer engines — Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT browsing, and Perplexity. Makes your content the cited source when AI summarizes answers for your target queries.

Strategic SEO Planning

Tailored 90-day to 12-month SEO roadmaps built for your business model — SaaS, local, eCommerce, publishing, or agency. Covers keyword architecture, content calendars, link-building strategy, and KPI targets.

Programmatic SEO

Identifies opportunities to scale content through templated, data-driven page generation — location pages, comparison pages, product variants — so you can rank for thousands of long-tail queries efficiently.

Competitor Comparison Pages

Generates high-converting "vs" and alternative pages by analyzing competitor positioning, extracting differentiation angles, and building SEO-optimised comparison content that captures bottom-of-funnel intent.

Hreflang & International SEO

Audits and generates hreflang tags for multi-language and multi-region sites — detecting mismatches, missing return tags, and incorrect locale codes, then producing a corrected implementation plan.
